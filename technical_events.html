FPV RACING

FPV, also called first person view drones, are aerial vehicles fitted with cameras that live stream the video to the operator’s headset. The participants control and race through a course and compete against each other for the top spot.

ROBO RACE

The participants design a robot either wired or wireless within specified dimensions that operate manually and can traverse through all turns of the track where they race against time and other competitors.

LINE FOLLOWER

The line follower robot is an autonomous mobile machine that can detect and follow a black line or a strip made on the floor within a specified margin of error. Robot finishing in the least time wins.

DIRT RUSH

Dirt Rush is a form of RC car racing that takes place on dirt tracks while navigating obstacles such as jumps, berms, and whoops in the least time possible.

MAZE SOLVER

A maze solver RC competition involves radio-controlled vehicles that are specifically designed to navigate through complex mazes autonomously and as quickly as possible.

ROBO WARS

Robo Wars is a thrilling event in which robots built by competitors fight each other in a controlled arena. Robots are usually armed and the goal is to disable or destroy their opponents’ robots.
